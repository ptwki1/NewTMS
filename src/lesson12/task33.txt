There are five types of schools in the US educational system.
 They are: kindergarten, elementary school, middle school, high school and private school.
 Children go to kindergarten when they are 5 years old.
 They go to elementary school from ages 6 through 11 (1-5 grades),
 middle school from ages 12 through 14 (6-8 grades) and high school from ages 15 through 19 (9-12 grades).
